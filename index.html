Intro

Welcome to this website! This website is for people who have or people who are helping some one else through cancer. Or any ailment for that matter. It is Important for everyone to talk about their mental health. The video below really emphasises this point. Mental health is something that is being brought to the for forefront of society. People going through illness have a higher likelyhood to have a bad mental health. In fact 1/3 Cancer patients experience a mental health concern, during or after treatment. The link between cancer specifically and mental health has been sutdied alot and is well understood. In fact it raises the total healthcare cost by 45% for some one with a prolonged condition. This is a cost that the patient doesnt have to worry about in ireland because Cancer treatment is free for all citizens. This website is made to be an extension of the Irish cancer society's website and documents some tactics that can be used to help prevent a dip in mental health. The header includes quick links to the different sections of the website for easy navigation. It also has links to places that can help with a person suffering from a dip in mental health or a prolongued illness. The final section on the header has a link to donate to the ICS and help support cancer research and its patients. Below you can finde a video describing how important it is to talk about mental health. After this you'll find a gallery describing the activities previously mentioned, and finally a form to contact us about things that made your mental health better when going through a tough time. If you could fill this section out before leaving the website that would be much appreciated. Have a great day and try to enjoy it.

Gallery for good mental health

Having visits from family members, loved ones or friends can greatly help with ones mood and can only serve to benefit your mental health. Don't be afraid to ask someone to visit you if you feel like you want to see them or even just to talk to someone.

In our modern world of technology it would be a shame if we didnt use it to our advantage to do some things we enjoy. Be it pleying some video games, Messaging friends or even just simply watching some youtube or a show. These things can all be very enjoyable and may serve to distract one from a bad slump or just waste some time in hospital.

This may seem fairly obvious, but keeping on top of simple things like medication, eatign and drinking all help to avoid stress and ensure that you can keep a healthy mindset moving onto your next problem or task.

Excerising and stetching are extremely important for cancer patients. The old saying healthy body is a healthy mind isnt wrong by any means. Making sure muscles dont decay is very important and excersise releases endorphines which allow a person to keep happy and stay healthy.

Though Simalar to the last activity. It is very impoortant to get outside for walks or for exercise or just for some fresh air. In states of immuno- suppression it may be advisable to ignore this activity. But when possible please ensure that you get out of you house or hospital room for a short walk out in the air.

It is also important recognise What thoughts are good and what are bad. Some of these on the left are good mindsets to have while going through treatments. Most hospitals have a therapist or at very least someone to talk to. Employ these ways of thinking and use the options that have presented themselves to your advantage.
